Rules, How to Enter and the Prize Process

If you signed up with Opacity through the Opacity registration you will automatically be entered into the prize draws if you have the given amount of points by the end of the month.

If you signed up to Opacity using Facebook, in order to enter the prize draw you must email prizes@opacityapp.com indicating your username to us. The reason you cannot automatically be entered is because we do not have your email when logging in through Facebook so if you win we need a way to contact you.

If you are using Facebook, you only need to send us an email for the very first prize. We will automatically add it to the database so you can automatically be entered into future prize draws.

Prizes will close on the last day of the month at 23:59. We will compile a list of all the users entered into the draw and run a random number generator and will email the winner of the prize. The winner will have 3 business days to accept the prize. If the winner does not accept the prize in the 3 days, another winner will be chosen. If you have any questions or concerns about the prizes and the process, please feel free to email prizes@opacityapp.com
